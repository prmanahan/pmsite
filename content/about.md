I'm a Technical Program Manager and Software Architect based in Fredericton, New Brunswick, with a long career at IBM Canada Software Lab spanning software development, technical leadership, and cloud platform launches.

I specialize in streamlining operations through DevOps, automation, trunk-based development, and data-driven methodologies. Most recently I led initiatives optimizing CI/CD pipelines and developer productivity for IBM's QRadar security intelligence platform — taking builds from daily failures to rare monthly occurrences and accelerating quality deliveries from quarterly to weekly.

Before that I served as Technical Program Manager and product owner for QRadar and QRadarSuite, launching the SaaS offering, establishing a developer hub with Backstage, and aligning cross-functional teams across development, support, architecture, and SRE. Earlier roles include managing Agile teams on back-end services (database, API, Big Data), leading the Q1 Labs acquisition integration into IBM Security, and working on IBM Rational tools and VisualAge for Java.

Outside of work, I enjoy building things with Rust — factory game calculators, game engine experiments with Bevy, and exploring AI-assisted development workflows.
